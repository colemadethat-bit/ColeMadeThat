About Me

My name is Cole Adams. I’m located in Bucks County, Pennsylvania. I started ColeMadeThat to help my brother out with his brand, and I’ve continued to serve and help local brands in my county ever since.

I specialize in high-quality product labels for honey brands, hot sauce companies, coffee roasters, and other consumable startups—but I offer plenty of other printing services too, including banners and more. I’ve worked with a couple of local farms and I’m always expanding small business partnerships.

I started out with an HP Latex printer and a Sumida D-Series 160 Cutter, and I’ve been growing from there. Whether you’re looking to launch a new product or refresh your lineup, I handle labels, stickers, banners, and packaging with a hands-on approach and fast turnaround.
